# Photosynthesis Sample Notes

## Big Idea
Plants convert light energy into chemical energy stored as glucose.

## Key Vocabulary
- Photosynthesis
- Chlorophyll
- Glucose
- Carbon dioxide
- Oxygen

## Lecture Guidance
Use these notes to explain how sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide become glucose and oxygen.

## Closing Point
Photosynthesis supports most food chains and helps maintain oxygen in Earth’s atmosphere.
